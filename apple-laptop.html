Jack - 21 Oct 2021

Great performance and excellent battery life. One thing to take into account is that a lot of X86-64 software is not yet compatible or optimised for for Apple Silicon. Apps built for Intel chips which run through Apple's Rosetta 2 emulator are sometimes a bit laggy, but this is just a short-term issue. Also just a reminder that these MacBooks only have 2 thunderbolt ports (no other ports at all), so you will probably need to get an adapter. The MacBook is also slightly heavier than I expected. Overall I am very pleased with the purchase.
